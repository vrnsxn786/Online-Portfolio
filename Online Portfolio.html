3)  X-Configuration Quadcopter

4)  RC Aircraft

A single motor powered radio-controlled aircraft made using coroplast sheet to overcome drag/pitch/roll forces. Wings were made of highly compressed thermocol and 3 servo motors were used to control ailerons, radar and pitch.

5)  Line Follower

Autonomous robot which follows either a black line in white area or a white line in black area. Robot must be able to detect particular line and keep following it. to the car during its flight.

6)  Rhino Bot 1.0/2.0/3.0

It was a “wedge” and “drum” design robot working on simple mechanical principles. A simple spinning drum above a wedge is placed at the front side of the “Rhino Bot” for lifting and hitting the opponent. We designed the remote control system using the principle of reversing the polarity of terminals of power source.
